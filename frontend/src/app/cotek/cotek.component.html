About Our Company

Where We Came From


Founded in 2022 as an outsourcing company and a creative agency, Cotek Company offers various services such as graphic design, digital communication, software development, web and mobile development or software architecture consulting.

The goal of Cotek is to provide a comprehensive set of services for its customers. It aims to create the best possible match between clients’ market niche and their target audience. This is achieved this by implementing creative and innovative marketing solutions.

Our Goal


Creativity is in our DNA, people are our inspiration and technology is our playground.
We offer simple solutions to complex issues, always following the same principle: creating value for the customer and the consumer.

In a changing world, Cotek Company adapts its services and expertise to better support its partners and turn good ideas into meaningful experiences and services because the quality work is what makes it stand out in regard to our competitors on the market.
